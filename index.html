    Empowering young minds
    Transforming education for a brighter future

    Myriam foundation is a dedicated NGO in Côte d’Ivoire, providing educational support to children and youth aged 3 to 25. We focus on promoting education and language skills through engaging activities such as English language initiation, cultural and artistic events, and personal development programs. Our mission is to empower the next generation by equipping them with the knowledge and skills necessary for success in an ever-changing world.
